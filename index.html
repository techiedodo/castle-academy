Common Questions

Our frequently asked questions, answered.

Is Castle Academy a licensed facility?

Yes, we are licensed by the Department of Children and Family Services.

How are teachers chosen?

Castle Academy staff is selected for their expertise, knowledge and commitment to teaching young children. All staff members must meet or supersede the requirements of Castle Academy of Early Education and the Department of Children and Family Services.

Is ongoing teacher training required?

Yes, a minimum of 15 hours of professional development training is required per school year. In addition, all staff members must complete regular health examinations and background checks, which includes fingerprinting. Classes are also taken in CPR, first aid, preventing infectious diseases, blood borne pathogens and food allergies.

How many classrooms are there?

There are five early childhood classrooms.  Children are placed according to desired program,  age and availability.

Is there an outside play area?

Yes, there is a secured outside play area exclusively for Castle Academy students.

Is parent involvement encouraged?

Yes, we always welcome parents to celebrate their children with us by attending classroom functions, field trips, and participating in guest reader/chef programs.

What is your discipline policy?

Castle Academy guidance is always positive and the teachers resolve conflicts through teaching and redirecting.
